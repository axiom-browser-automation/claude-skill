Open the axiom.ai extension, go to the dashboard, and click the burger menu in the top-right. Select **My account** to open your account settings.

![The axiom.ai account menu opened from the dashboard](/docs/tutorials/axiom-account-menu.jpg)

## Set up a team account
***

A team account shares a single subscription and runtime allocation between everyone in the team. Team accounts are available on the Ultimate plan only, and we set them up manually within 1 to 2 business days of receiving the request.

To request a team account:

1. Click **Account** in the main menu, accessed from the top-left of the dashboard.
2. In the **Typeform**, enter the email address of every account you want included in the team plan.
3. Click **Submit**.

A team will be in touch within 1 to 2 business days to finish the setup.

![The team account request form in axiom.ai](/docs/team-account-axiom-ai.webp)

## Change email
***

1. Click **Account** in the main menu, accessed from the top-left of the dashboard.
2. Click **Email & password**.
3. Replace the value in `Email` with the new email address.
4. Click **Update account**.

![The email address field in the axiom.ai account settings](/docs/tutorials/axiom-email-account.jpg)

## Change password
***

1. Click **Account** in the main menu, accessed from the top-left of the dashboard.
2. Click **Email & password**.
3. Enter your existing password in `Current password`.
4. Enter your new password in `New password`.
5. Re-enter the new password in `Retype new password`. This must match `New password` exactly.
6. Click **Change password**.

![The change password form in the axiom.ai account settings](/docs/tutorials/change-paasword.jpg)

## Change company name and role
***

1. Click **Account** in the main menu, accessed from the top-left of the dashboard.
2. Enter the new value in `Enter company name`.
3. Enter the new value in `Enter company role`.
4. Click **Update account**.

## Change your country
***

1. Click **Account** in the right-side menu.
2. Select your country from `Select country`.
3. Click **Update account**.

## Delete your account
***

> **Warning:** Account deletion is permanent. Once an account is deleted, it can't be recovered.

1. If you have an active plan, [unsubscribe first](/docs/no-code-tool/account/subscription).
2. Click **Delete my account**.

![The delete account section in the axiom.ai account settings](/docs/tutorials/delete-account.jpg)
