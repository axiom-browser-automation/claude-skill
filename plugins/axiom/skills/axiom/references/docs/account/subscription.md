Manage your axiom.ai subscription to upgrade your plan, add runtime, update billing details, or download invoices. For account settings outside billing, see [manage your account](/docs/no-code-tool/account/manage-account).

## Change your plan
***

Open the axiom.ai Chrome extension and navigate to the **Change plan** section.

![The Change plan button in the axiom.ai extension](/docs/tutorials/chnage-plan-button.jpg)

The **Change plan** section shows every plan we offer. Click **Change plan** on the plan you want to switch to.

![Selecting a new plan from the change plan list](/docs/tutorials/chnage-plans-select.jpg)

## Manage billing in the payment portal
***

Update your payment method, billing information, and download invoices from the [payment portal](https://billing.stripe.com/p/login/5kA16u1uibP19bO6oo). The portal is provided by [Stripe](https://stripe.com), our payment partner.

![The Stripe payment portal opened from axiom.ai](/docs/tutorials/stripe-portal.jpg)

To open the portal, follow the [payment portal link](https://billing.stripe.com/p/login/5kA16u1uibP19bO6oo) and enter the email address you used to create your axiom.ai account. Stripe sends a one-time link to that address; click the link in the email to enter the portal.

## Update your payment method
***

You can add or update payment methods on your account at any time.

1. In the payment portal, click **Add payment method**.
2. Choose **Card** or **Google Pay**.
3. Click **Add**.

> **Note:** If you'd prefer to pay by invoice, contact [support@axiom.ai](mailto:support@axiom.ai).

## Update your billing information
***

Update your billing information at any time. This is useful for changes to billing name, address, or VAT details.

1. In the payment portal, click **Update information**.
2. Edit the fields you need to change.
3. Click **Save**.

## Download invoices
***

Past invoices and receipts are available in the payment portal.

1. In the payment portal, find the **Invoice history** section.
2. Click the date of the invoice you want.
3. Click **Download** to download the invoice, the receipt, or both.
