## What to use the Send an email step for
***

Use this step to send an email to one or more email addresses. Very useful for confirming an Axiom has run or sharing data scraped from a reporting page with colleagues. Please respect this step and not use it to spam others.

You can use this step to:

- Send confirmations an Axiom has completed
- Email colleagues data you have scraped
- Distribute files you have downloaded

## How to configure the Send an email step
***

### Email addresses

Enter a list of email addresses to send to, one email address per line.

All emails are sent from a no-reply axiom.ai email address and you will not receive a reply to any message sent. Messages are rate limited to one per 30 seconds. Please do not use this step to send unsolicited email to third parties!

### Email subject

Add your email subject line in this field.

### Email body

Add your email body in this field. Use 'Insert Data' to pass data from any step into your email body.
